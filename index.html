General Tips for all exercises

  • Do negative reps after reaching muscle failure.
  • Controlled, steady movements prevent [using momentum to cheat exercises].
  • Mentally focus on muscles being pumped to develop mind-muscle connection.
  • Rest for as little time as possible between reps so as to maintain muscle tension and heart rate.
  • "Stop counting reps. Make your reps count." -Jeff Cavaliere from Athlean X
  • Muscle growth begins when muscles start to fail.
  • Warming up (increasing heart rate) & streching increase blood flow and loosen muscles, helping prevent injury.
  • Hold posture for a few seconds at peak contraction.
    • Muscle and Fitness
    • Adrian Bryant Youtube
  • Lengthening (via stretching) pumped muscles during rest periods increases muscle hypertrophy.
    • Frontiers in Sports and Active Living literature: Inter-set stretch
